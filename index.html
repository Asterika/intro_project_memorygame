Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down
on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn.

The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Play?

Keep your synapses snapping with this rapid-fire memory test!

Surpass your own stats or best your buds in this captivating cognitive challenge.
